Welcome

Welcome to my commission site. I draw variety of stuff from humans to monstergirls to furries to semi-complex robots... You're feel free to reach out to me whether I can work on your character or not. I can draw suggestive things and light kinks. You can click on each piece to check my pricing. Commissions will be paid via Ko-fi, VGen, PayPal or other stuff (Steam games etc.). I will NOT accept cryptocurrency or any form of blockchain content as payment.

For commissions, unfortunately I do not update my commission status very often. If you wish to know my status please ask me through Discord or Bluesky. I try my best to regularly inform and update people about my queue and if I'm working on a piece. Once it is your turn, I very much regularly send you the work-in-progress sketches and alike to ensure the piece is up to your liking. The turnaround is about a week once the sketch is complete.

Terms of Service

  /\_/\  
 ( o.o ) 
> ^ <
  • YOU MUST BE OVER 18 YEARS OF AGE TO COMMISSION ME.
  • I have full rights to refuse a commission, regardless whether I give you a reason or not.
  • You are allowed to ask for a refund. Depending on the progress, you will get your refund. However, I would like you to be rather patient with me about sending the refund.
  • You are feel free to use my art as profile pictures, banners, decoration, icons or even real life merch (without profiting from it). You don't need to take permission for this.
  • You are NOT allowed to turn them into NFTs or any type of blockchain/crypto material. If you commission me just to turn my art into an NFT, you will be blacklisted forever. This includes any type of blockchain profit.
  • You are not allowed to feed my art into an AI or put it on a re-drawing AI image or whatever the fuck. Do not use my art for any type of AI generated content. If I catch you doing this, you'll be blacklisted forever.
  • You cannot sell my art as merch to profit from other people without my permission. However, you can turn my art into keychains, standees, prints etc for personal/non-profit use.
  • I am allowed to use the piece I made for you as commission/art examples. To ensure, I will never claim the character as mine if I ever do so.
  • If you get a custom design from me, you are feel free to use it as a mascot, make a costume/fursuit/plushies etc. of it and profit from it.
  • You are allowed to resell/trade custom designs, only if you price the original piece not too above their worth (excluding extra art, in this case it can be above what you paid for me and it is completely fine if you price it above what you paid for me). Though, I advice waiting a little bit before selling the character.
  • You are allowed to ask for the unwatermarked piece, just do not claim the art as your own and be careful about a potential art theft (credit me if posting unwatermarked)
